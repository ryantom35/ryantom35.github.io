Projects for UC Berkeley's Fall 2025 Intro to Computer Vision & Computational Photography

Ryan Tom

Project 0: Becoming Friends with Your Camera

Introductory camera studies: selfies, perspective compression, and a dolly zoom demo.

Project 1: Images of the Russian Empire

Taking digitized Prokudin-Gorskii glass plate images and, using image processing techniques, automatically produce a color image.

Project 2: Fun with Filters and Frequencies

Exploring convolution, edge detection, image sharpening, hybrid images, Gaussian/Laplacian stacks, and multiresolution blending techniques.
